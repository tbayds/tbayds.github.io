Frequently Asked Questions

What types of products can I find at TrendBay?

At TrendBay, we offer a wide range of quality groceries, fresh farm produce, and premium poultry products. Our selection includes fresh vegetables, fruits, chicken, eggs, and various grocery items to meet all your culinary needs.

How can I place an order?

You can easily place an order through our supermarket, mart.trendbayds.com. Simply browse our products, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. We offer a seamless online shopping experience.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we currently deliver to Lagos and Abuja. Once you place your order, our team will ensure it is delivered to your doorstep promptly and in excellent condition.

How can I contact customer service?

For general inquiries: hello@trendbayds.com For support: You can reach our customer service team via email at help@trendbayds.com or through our contact page on the website. We are here to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and mobile payments. Our checkout process is secure and easy to use.

What is your return policy?

Due to the perishable nature of our products, we do not accept returns. However, if you experience any issues with your order, such as receiving damaged or incorrect items, please contact us within 24 hours of receiving your order. We will address the issue promptly to ensure your satisfaction.
